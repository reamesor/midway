🎲 Colors — Game Rules

How to Play

Bet on up to 3 colors from 6: Yellow, Orange, Pink, Blue, Green, Red. Three dice roll at once. Win if any of your colors match the dice.

Example: bet Blue + Green, dice show Blue, Blue, Red → 2 matches, you win!

Payouts (5% house edge)

1 match: Bet + (Bet × 1.04)
2 matches: Bet + (Bet × 1.04 × 2)
3 matches (Jackpot): Bet + (Bet × 4.5)

Each color costs your full bet amount.

The Midway Twist

Unlike a normal arcade, the 5% house edge doesn't vanish. Every roll routes it into the shared Treasury → 40% buyback & burn, 40% believers' pool, 20% build fund. The edge comes home.

Fun Mode: virtual points, 1000 starting balance, risk-free.
